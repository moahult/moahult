Poster

This poster was created during the autumn semester of 2023 as part of a school project. Inspired by Saul Bass, the design emphasizes strong color contrasts and an asymmetrical composition. Hierarchy was established through size contrasts between images and text, placement of key information in the optical center, and the use of whitespace. A sans-serif typeface was chosen for its clarity and suitability for short, impactful messages. The poster aims to communicate the essence of the film through a blend of visuals and typography, creating a compelling and dynamic narrative.
