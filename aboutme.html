section of text about me

Hello, I’m Moa Hult, a digital design student currently studying at Högskolan Kristanstad. I’m passionate about graphic design and UI/UX design, with a strong focus on creating modern, simplistic designs that incorporate bold colors. Exploring how AI can shape the future of creative experiences is something that intrigues me as well.

Before pursuing digital design, I worked as a chef, an experience that taught me adaptability and fostered an open-minded approach to working with people from diverse backgrounds. This perspective inspires me to place users and customers at the center of my design process, ensuring their needs and insights drive every project I undertake.

When I’m not designing, you’ll likely find me drawing or painting, experimenting in the kitchen or out in nature exploring. My goal is to work in a creative, collaborative environment where I can grow as a designer and contribute to impactful designs with a team, users, or companies. I’m always excited to connect with like-minded individuals, so feel free to reach out if you’d like to collaborate or discuss creative ideas!
