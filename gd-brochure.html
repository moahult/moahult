Brand Identity Design

This project was created during the spring semester of 2024 and allowed me to develop a complete visual identity for a fictional vegetarian food company, tof&temp. Drawing inspiration from bold packaging and vibrant designs, I used a high-contrast color palette of green and orange to reflect the brand's fresh, plant-based focus. A panda illustration became the central graphic element, representing the brand's playful and modern personality.

Throughout the project, I worked on various design elements, including packaging, business cards, and a brand pitch presentation. The packaging was designed to balance symmetry and dynamism through centered compositions and bold illustrations that extend beyond the margins. Typography choices combined elegant serifs for readability in body text and geometric sans-serifs for clarity in headings, creating a cohesive and modern visual identity.
